🚗 Essential Traffic Rules for Safe City Driving 🚗

🛑Traffic Signals
Always obey traffic lights and signals. At a red light, stop completely before the white line and wait until green. Yellow means prepare to stop, not accelerate.
⚠️Speed Limits
Adhere to posted speed limits at all times. In residential areas, maintain 25 mph unless otherwise posted. School zones require reduced speeds during operational hours.
🚸Pedestrian Priority
Always yield to pedestrians at crosswalks, whether marked or unmarked. When a pedestrian has entered a crosswalk, vehicles must stop and remain stopped until they've crossed.
📱Distracted Driving
Using handheld mobile devices while driving is prohibited. Use hands-free systems for calls and never text while driving. Pull over safely if you need to use your phone.
🚑Emergency Vehicles
When emergency vehicles with flashing lights and sirens approach, pull to the right edge of the road and stop until they've passed. Never block intersections.
🅿️Parking Regulations
Park only in designated areas. Do not park within 15 feet of a fire hydrant, in front of driveways, or on crosswalks. Always check for parking restrictions and time limits.
